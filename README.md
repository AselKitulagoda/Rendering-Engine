# Running Instructions

```make fast``` or ```make speedy``` to run the optimised version

## Rendering Modes

### Wireframe
* Press **J** to view wireframe render
* Press **5** to toggle *Wu Lines Mode* on wireframe

### Rasteriser
* Press **K** to view rasterise render

### Raytracer
* Press **L** to view raytrace render
* Press **M** to view raytrace SSAA render
* Press **O** to toggle *Hard Shadows Mode*
* Press **1** to toggle *Soft Shadows Mode*
* Press **2** to toggle *Mirrors/Reflective surfaces*
* Press **3** to toggle *Gouraud Shading* 
* Press **4** to toggle *Phong Shading*
* Press **B** to toggle *Metallic surfaces*
* Press **6** to toggle *Refraction mode* (PARTIALLY IMPLEMENTED)

### Camera
* WASD to *tilt/pan* camera
* F/G to rotate camera along Z
* Arrow keys to *move* camera (X-Y)
* Z/X to move the camera towards and away (Z)
* Press **E** for camera + lookAt + orbit
* Press **R** to *Reset camera and light*
* Press **C** to *Clear the screen*

### Animation
* Press **Q** for *shake* animation
* Press **7** for *bounce* animation
* Press **8** for *bounce and squash* animation
* Press **9** for *fade in* animation
* Press **P** to save the window frame into the **outputs/** folder

All the ```.mp4``` rendered animations can be found in the ```animations/``` folder.
